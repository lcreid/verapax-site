Welcome to Verapax.
We work for social change and transformational learning.
We work with you to build and advance structural changes that support social justice, equity, diversity and inclusion, locally and globally.

“Our clients tell us that a fancy-looking written evaluation report at the end of a project is not enough. They want strong evaluation services that go beyond documenting performative outcomes.

They want their staff, stakeholders, and project beneficiaries to be invested in the evaluation journey.

What they want is an evaluation framework and tools that are conducive to self-reflection, dialogue, and systems thinking.

What they need is evaluation for transformational outcomes.“

~ Angela M. Contreras. Director.

Verapax (n):

  • True peace. Peace and truth.
  • Verapaz – Region where two natural symbols of peace and freedom can be found. The Monja Blanca and the Quetzal – Guatemala’s official national flower and bird.
  • The total package of mission-based customized research, analysis, management and evaluation solutions, service and advice used to deliver the idea of a company or deliverable into the hands of a customer.

Verapax is a small, boutique consultancy.

Verapax was founded by Angela Contreras, Director and Principal Consultant. Our work is informed by our lived experiences of being racialized by mainstream society and navigating systemic barriers to justice, equality, diversity and inclusion.

We service small to mid-sized not-for-profit organizations and institutions of teaching and learning. We work with our clients to develop organizational leadership and evaluative thinking throughout the processes of designing, implementing, and evaluating their initiatives.
