Verapax

Collaborative, participatory, empowering, and transformational approaches to project management, research, and evaluation.

Welcome to Verapax.
We work for social change and transformational learning.
We work with you to build and advance structural changes that support social justice, equity, diversity and inclusion, locally and globally.

“Our clients tell us that a fancy-looking written evaluation report at the end of a project is not enough. They want strong evaluation services that go beyond documenting performative outcomes.

They want their staff, stakeholders, and project beneficiaries to be invested in the evaluation journey.

What they want is an evaluation framework and tools that are conducive to self-reflection, dialogue, and systems thinking.

What they need is evaluation for transformational outcomes.“

~ Angela M. Contreras. Director.
