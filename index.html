So what and who is Verapax anyway?

Verapax (n):

  • True peace. Peace and truth.
  • Verapaz - Region where two natural symbols of peace and freedom can be found. The Monja Blanca and the Quetzal – Guatemala’s official national flower and bird.
  • The total package of mission-based customized research, analysis, management and evaluation solutions, service and advice used to deliver the idea of a company or deliverable into the hands of a customer.

Verapax Solutions is a small, boutique consultancy headed by applied project management and evaluation expert Angela Contreras. We service small to mid-sized not-for-profit organizations from the socio-legal services sectors. We help our customers build upon their ideas in measurable and strategic steps that will result successful projects, programs, organizations. Our high quality planning, monitoring and evaluation solutions, service and advice is customer-centered. We focus on the intersection between our clients’ mission and vision, their strategic planning, their resources and goals, the interests of the communities they serve, and the interests of their funding partners.
